• Welcome to SnowBrawl!
  • You play as the Bearling; dodge the snowballs coming from the left and right of the screen.

  • Be careful though! Snowballs are aimed at either your upper or lower body and get fast very quickly.

  • Jump and duck appropriately, and go for a high score!!

  • Controlling the Bearling:
  • 'A' to move left. 'S' to duck. 'D' to move right. 'Spacebar' to jump.

  • Press Enter to start the game!

  • Pro Tip: Full screen your browser for optimal performance!
